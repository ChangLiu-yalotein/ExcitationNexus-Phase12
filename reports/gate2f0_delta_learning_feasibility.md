# Gate 2-F0 delta-learning feasibility

Semantically matched pairs exist for HOMO, LUMO, gap (hartree), and dipole magnitude (debye), with minimum coverage 1.000000. Signed deltas are frozen as DFT minus PM6. Delta/DFT weighted standard-deviation ratios are homo_hartree=0.556, lumo_hartree=0.630, gap_hartree=0.652, dipole_magnitude_debye=0.440; these describe numerical stability only and do not establish predictive value. Energy delta is rejected because PM6 reported energy and DFT SCF energy are different physical quantities; coordinate dipole deltas are rejected as non-invariant. Core D/A interface distances cover 0.974227, below the 0.99 family threshold because all 387 empty-donor records are retained without imputation. Admitted deltas require Tier 2 DFT at deployment; PM6-only deployment cannot obtain them. A later capacity-controlled validation-only experiment can compare C0, C0+ground-state scalars, C0+role/interface descriptors, and C0+paired deltas without any TDDFT input.

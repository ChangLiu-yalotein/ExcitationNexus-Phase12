# Gate 3-A1 ranking stability

One frozen deployment model and 20 fixed structure-group bootstrap models scored 36,523 candidates once. The 1% extreme-pool stability gate (frequency at least 80%) contains 6 low-proxy and 98 high-proxy candidates. Bootstrap quantiles, rank frequency, and standard deviation describe ranking stability only; they are not conformal, calibrated, confidence, prediction, or experimental intervals.
